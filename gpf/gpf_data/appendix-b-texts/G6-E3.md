|           | Monday    | Tuesday    | Wednesday   | Thursday   |
|:----------|:----------|:-----------|:------------|:-----------|
| Morning   | Fish      | Fish       | Fish        | Meat       |
| Morning   | Ice       | -          | Ice         | Milk       |
| Afternoon | Batteries | Fruit      | Flour       | Fruit      |
| Afternoon | Soap      | Vegetables | Dried Beans | Vegetables |
| Afternoon | Candles   | Tea        | Rice        | Rice       |
| Afternoon | Rope      | Coffee     | Sugar       | Candles    |
